Mango Salsa Ingredients

  • Mango
  • Vegetables
  • Cilantro
  • Citrus juice

How To Make Fresh mango Salsa

  1. Mix all the ingregients,then let sit for about half an hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  2. Mix ingredients well to combine.Cover and let sit at least 30 minutes before serving.
  3. Serve with chips.
