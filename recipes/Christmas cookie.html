Ingredients used in Christmas Cookie

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • salt
  • Sugar and margarine
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla

How To Make Christmass Cookie

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the margarine and sugar in another bowl,then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Chill the dough in the fridge.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into desired sshapes.
  5. Bake the cookies on prepared baking sheets until the adges are golden.
